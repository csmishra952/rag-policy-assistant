**TechGadget Inc. Refund Policy**
Last Updated: 2024-01-15

1. Standard Return Window
Customers may return products within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund. 
However, for "Open Box" or "Refurbished" items, the return window is strictly 14 days.

2. Condition of Items
Items must be in original condition. Electronics must not have been powered on for more than 2 hours total usage time (checked via system logs). 

3. Non-Refundable Items
- Digital software keys once redeemed.
- Headphones (for hygiene reasons) if the seal is broken.
- Gift cards.

4. Refund Processing Time
Once we receive your return, allow 5-7 business days for inspection. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 3-5 business days after approval.
