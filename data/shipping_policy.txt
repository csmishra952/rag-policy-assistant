**TechGadget Inc. Shipping Policy**

1. Shipping Zones
We currently ship to the US, Canada, and the UK. We do NOT ship to P.O. Boxes in Canada.

2. Free Shipping Eligibility
Orders over $50 USD qualify for free standard shipping (5-7 business days).
Orders under $50 USD are charged a flat rate of $5.99.

3. Expedited Shipping
- 2-Day Shipping: $12.99
- Overnight Shipping: $24.99 (Order must be placed before 2 PM EST).

4. Lost Packages
If a package is marked "Delivered" but you have not received it, you must report it within 48 hours. We are not responsible for package theft after confirmed delivery.
