**TechGadget Inc. Privacy & Data Policy**

1. Data Collection
We collect email addresses, shipping addresses, and device usage statistics. We do NOT store credit card numbers; these are handled by our payment processor, Stripe.

2. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share shipping data with FedEx/UPS solely for delivery purposes.

3. Account Deletion
You may request account deletion by emailing privacy@techgadget.inc. This process takes up to 30 days.
